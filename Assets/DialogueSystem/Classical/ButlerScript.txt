ASTRONAUT
Hello! Are you the butler of the house?


BUTLER
Why yes, I am indeed. Good evening [or whatever time of day the sky is]. How may I be of assistance?


ASTRONAUT
I was wondering, what’s wrong with this planet? The gardener mentioned a “terrible evil” and the maid said that all of the music had disappeared. 


BUTLER
Ah, yes. They do not exaggerate. Once, this was a prosperous land, filled with the delicate sound of music. But now, evil has taken our music away and we have lost our ways. Say, would you be in a position to help us out a little?


ASTRONAUT
Sure, what can I do?


BUTLER
Inside this mansion behind me is one of the adjutants of the Evil One. Take this powerful weapon and destroy them. We will forever be in your debt.
